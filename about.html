About Cowork SG

Built by practitioners,
for practitioners.

Cowork SG is a not-for-profit community website made by non-technical practitioners, for non-technical practitioners — heavy users of Claude and other AI tools who share what actually works.

What we are

Cowork SG is a free, not-for-profit community. We don't sell courses, subscriptions, or certifications. Everything we produce — guides, prompt templates, workflow breakdowns, blog posts — is shared openly at no cost.

We exist because the gap between "AI is transforming everything" and "I actually know how to use it in my day job" is still enormous for most non-technical professionals. We're trying to close that gap, one practical workflow at a time.

Who we're for

Non-technical professionals who are serious about using AI tools — not just experimenting with them. People who use Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or other AI tools daily and want to go deeper. People who share what they learn rather than hoarding it.

  • HR professionals automating candidate screening and onboarding
  • Consultants building proposal and research workflows
  • Marketing and comms teams running content pipelines
  • Business owners and founders doing more with smaller teams
  • Anyone who wants to work smarter without writing code

Our values

🔓

Always free

No paywalls. No premium tiers. Every resource is open to everyone, forever.

🛠️

Practitioner-first

Everything we share comes from people who use these tools in their actual work — not instructors teaching theory.

🌐

Tool-agnostic

We use Claude heavily, but we share workflows for ChatGPT, Gemini, and whatever else works — no brand loyalty, just results.

The founder

W

Wan Wei Soh

Founder, Cowork SG · CEO, ANCHR AI Labs

Wan Wei has spent years working with AI tools as a non-technical practitioner and building communities around practical AI adoption in Singapore and across Southeast Asia. She founded Cowork SG on a simple belief: if you're willing to learn and explore on your own, information should be completely free.

For those who want more structured guidance, a faster path, or hands-on support, her team at ANCHR AI Labs offers that next level of help — so there's always a place to go when you're ready to go deeper.
